Manufacturing Hub

FORT WORTH FACILITY

The heart of the global F-35 program, Lockheed Martin's mile-long production line in Fort Worth, Texas produces the majority of all F-35 aircraft

F-35 Lightning II aircraft on the production line at Lockheed Martin's Fort Worth facility

THE WORLD'S LARGEST FIGHTER FACTORY

The Fort Worth production facility spans over 2.7 million square feet of manufacturing space dedicated to F-35 production, supporting the global partnership's demand for aircraft.

PRODUCTION CAPACITY

Up to 156 aircraft per year at full rate production

WORKFORCE

Over 18,000 skilled employees dedicated to the program

SMART FACTORY

Industry 4.0 automated systems with augmented reality for assembly

ECONOMIC IMPACT

The Fort Worth facility generates over $10 billion in annual economic impact for Texas and supports more than 55,000 indirect jobs through its supply chain.

ADVANCED MANUFACTURING

Robotic painting systems apply radar-absorbing materials with precision accuracy impossible to achieve by hand.

DIGITAL THREAD

Each aircraft is tracked digitally from first component to final delivery, ensuring perfect parts alignment and lifecycle management.

GLOBAL INTEGRATION

Components arrive from partners worldwide for final assembly, with 70-80% of each aircraft's value coming from the extended supplier network.

FEATURED FACILITY: CAMERI FACO

The Final Assembly and Check-Out (FACO) facility in Cameri, Italy is one of only two such facilities outside the United States. This state-of-the-art manufacturing center produces F-35A and F-35B aircraft for the Italian and Dutch armed forces.

  • 1 million square feet of production space
  • Capacity to produce up to 8 aircraft per year
  • Also produces wing assemblies for the entire F-35 program
